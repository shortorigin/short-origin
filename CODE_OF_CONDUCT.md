# Code of Conduct

Short Origin expects contributors to collaborate professionally and constructively.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in reviews, issues, pull requests, and design discussions.
- Critique code and decisions directly; do not attack people.
- Share context, risks, and tradeoffs clearly.
- Assume good intent while still holding the technical bar high.
- Help keep repository discussions focused on concrete engineering outcomes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or exclusionary conduct.
- Repeated disruption of issue, review, or release workflows.
- Publishing private or security-sensitive information without authorization.

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit, lock, or remove content and may restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
